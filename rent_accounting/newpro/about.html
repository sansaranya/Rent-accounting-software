About Neyveli Township Administration

The Neyveli Township Administration is a dedicated wing of NLC India Limited (NLCIL), responsible for the comprehensive management and development of Neyveli Township. Established to support the vast industrial operations of NLCIL, the administration ensures a high quality of life for its residents by providing essential services and maintaining a sustainable environment. Our operations span over 54 sq. km, covering 32 distinct residential blocks.

Key Departments & Services

🌳 Horticulture & Environment

Managing Neyveli's extensive green cover, including numerous parks, gardens, and massive afforestation projects.

🏥 Health & Medical Services

Overseeing the operations of the NLCIL General Hospital (350+ beds) and various block-level dispensaries.

🛠 Civil & Infrastructure

Maintenance of over 300 km of roads, public buildings, and efficient water supply systems.

💡 Electrical Maintenance

Ensuring uninterrupted power supply to residential blocks and maintenance of street lighting.

                   

🏫 Education & Community Development

                   

Supporting educational institutions and fostering cultural activities through community halls and various developmental programs for residents.

               
                   

🗑 Public Health & Sanitation

                   

Managing waste collection, disposal, vector control, and overall township hygiene to ensure a clean and healthy living environment.
