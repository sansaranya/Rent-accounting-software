Rules and applicability for various rent types within the Neyveli Township:

TYPE OF RENT APPLICABLE TO
Normal Employees – Standard Rent for accommodation
Standard – A Retired Employees / Deceased Employees to whom retention of quarters till the end of academic year permitted on Educational Grounds, Employees for accommodation on sharing, etc.

EP-A (Employee Penal Rent-A) Penal Rent for Delayed vacation
EP-B (Employee Penal Rent-B) Penal Rent for Subletting / Overstayal and Other Violations
