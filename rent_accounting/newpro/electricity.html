Electricity Charges (Domestic)

As per the NLC India Limited employee policy, the following rates are applicable for domestic consumption in Neyveli Quarters:

Sl.No Unit Range Rate per Unit
1Up to 50 UnitsFREE
251 to 125 Units10 Paise
3126 to 200 Units15 Paise
4Above 200 UnitsSelling rate to TNEB

Neyveli Township Specifics

Direct Salary Recovery

Electricity charges are automatically calculated by the Township Administration and recovered from the monthly salary through the Pay & Accounts department.

High Consumption Clause

To promote energy conservation in the colony, any consumption exceeding 200 units is billed at the industrial selling rate provided to TNEB.

Maintenance Support

For meter reading discrepancies or electrical faults in quarters, employees should contact their respective Block Electrical Maintenance Unit.

Policy Revision

These rates are welfare-based and subject to periodic revision by NLC Management based on power generation costs and TNEB agreements.
